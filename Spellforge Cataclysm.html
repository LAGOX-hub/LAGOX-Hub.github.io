Spellforge Cataclysm

Unity - C# / 2D / Fantasy

In this game you control an evil knight wizard looking to kill everyone in the lands for a powerful artifact, cast magic spells or even mix the different elements to create new spells.

What I brought to the game was the player's walking, the enemies elemental resistances, making them take less damage from certain elements, and the level design.
