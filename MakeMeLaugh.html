Make Me Laugh

Unity - C# / Dark Humor / Thriller

A game jam project of 2024, the theme was "Make me laugh", the game revolves around making jokes to a monster that hides in the shadows of a dark comedy club, so keep it happy or meet a horrible end

I contributed to the camera movement, the text boxes the monster uses to talk to the player, and an inventory the player would use.
